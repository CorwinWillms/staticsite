Programs of Study

JMU Kinesiology Department's Website.

Exercise Science (B.S)

Students in the exercise science concentration will learn about physical activity in a way that is designed to help them gain the knowledge needed to be successful in a number of exercise involved health fields. The concentration requires courses in Anatomy, Physics, Chemisty, Pysiology and more. Graduates of this program will be able to enroll in the Health Fitness Instructor certification program.

PHETE Program (B.S., M.A.T)

The Physical & Health Education Teacher Education program is designed to educate health & physical education teachers. Students in this program must first be admitted into the teacher education program at JMU. Graduates of this program will receive a B.S. in Kinesiology at the end of their fourth year and a Master of Arts in Teaching at the end of their fifth year. This program contains all the prerequisites required to obtain a VA state teaching liscense.

Physiology (M.S.)

Students in this program receive specialized training in exercise physiology relating to physiological testing, exercise prescription and exercise research. This program includes a thesis and non-thesis track. Graduates of this program will obtain a M.S. in Kinesiology with a concentration in exercise physiology and will be prepared to enter the workplace in a variety of fitness and health industries or to pursue a doctoral degree.
