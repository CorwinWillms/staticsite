What is Kinesiology?

Kinesiology is the study of human movement. Students that major in Kinesiology at JMU engage with a number of different fields of study in the pursuit of a solid understanding of the human body and how it moves. Some of these fields include: biology, chemistry, health & behavioral sciences, physics and anatomy. Many undergraduate KIN majors at JMU will go on to further education at PA or PT school.
