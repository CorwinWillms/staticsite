Research Resources

JMU Libraries

The JMU library is a great place to find research papers to use as sources for your own papers or other coursework.

Google Scholar

Google scholar is another great place to search for research papers, although full access to many papers may not be free.

Study Resources

Khan Academy

Khan academy has free review material for the hard sciences.

ChemCollective

ChemCollective is a free site that contains chemistry course material for teachers and review material for students.

innerbody

innerbody research is a medical research and consulting company. On their website they provide interactive pages for reviewing human anatomy systems.

TeachMeAnatomy

TeachMeAnatomy is an anatomy study tool made specifically for healthcare students. It is a mobile app that can be found on the App Store or on GooglePlay.
